# Stephen Hawking Tribute Page

## Overview

This project is a tribute page dedicated to Stephen Hawking, one of the most influential theoretical physicists and cosmologists in history. The webpage highlights his life, achievements, contributions to science, and lasting legacy through a modern and responsive design.

## Features

* Modern dark space-themed user interface
* Responsive design for different screen sizes
* Biography section
* Major achievements section
* Books and contributions section
* Timeline of important events
* Legacy section
* Inspirational quotes
* Interactive hover effects

## Technologies Used

* HTML5
* CSS3
(I have used and taken help of AI tools)

## About Stephen Hawking

Stephen William Hawking (1942–2018) was a renowned theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author. Despite being diagnosed with ALS at a young age, he made groundbreaking contributions to our understanding of black holes, cosmology, and the universe. His work and determination continue to inspire millions around the world.
